Forks

A collaborative, distributed story.

Preface

You started this journey over a day ago. You left from your home in the countryside. It was a rainy day.

To help others follow you, you lay behind a winding path of corncockle cobblestones.

Chapter 1

dfannius observed you passing through the inlet where they lived and chose to follow. They travelled along the corncockle cobblestone path until the stones ran out. They planted instead a trail of tansy flowers for others to follow them. They set out on a heavy day.

worldwritable observed you passing through the bay where they lived and chose to follow. They travelled along the corncockle cobblestone path until the stones ran out. They planted instead a trail of tulip flowers for others to follow them. They set out on a foggy day.

thisisparker observed you passing through the farmland where they lived and chose to follow. They travelled along the corncockle cobblestone path until the stones ran out. They planted instead a trail of roses flowers for others to follow them. They set out on a rainy day.

Chapter 2
