Preface

You watched lizadaly passing through the bay where you live. They said they began this journey a day ago. You decided to join them yesterday on a foggy day.

They had laid a sturdy path of corncockle cobblestones until their pockets were empty. You don't carry stones, but instead planted behind you a trail of tulip flowers.

Chapter 1

threepress observed lizadaly passing through the inlet where they lived and chose to follow. They travelled along the corncockle cobblestone path until the stones ran out. They planted instead a trail of rosemary flowers for others to follow them. They set out on a heavy day.
